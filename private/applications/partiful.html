📖 Culture · Literary

Taiwan Travelogue Reading Night

Yáng Shuāng-zǐ's Taiwan Travelogue just won the 2026 International Booker Prize — the first novel translated from Mandarin to take the award. Structured as a 1930s culinary tour through Japanese-occupied Taiwan, it's the perfect anchor for a reading club: small group, assigned chapters, Taiwanese bites that mirror scenes in the book, and guided conversation on language, colonial history, and the love story at the center.

Current Trends

Twists on Current Trends

Six things I'd onboard this summer.

Right now 🏀 Knicks Watch Parties

The whole city is in Knicks fever. I keep ending up in rooms full of strangers yelling at one screen. Sports has quietly become the easiest reason to get people together this year and Partiful should be where those rooms get planned.

June 🌈 Pride Month

Pride is everywhere in June, but the moments I love are the small brunches and community walks, not the brand floats. The best events feel personal instead of corporate — that's the lens I'd bring to Explore curation.

Personal idea 🍷 Wine & Art Night

A personal event I'd love to host — wine, art, and a slow night out in the city. The Noguchi Museum is free on first Fridays all summer, open until 8pm on June 5, July 3, August 7, and September 4 — the kind of evening that deserves its own intimate Partiful invite.

Brand moment 💄 Rhode Summer Launch

Every Rhode drop feels less like a product launch and more like an event people plan around. I keep noticing "get ready with me" group hangs forming around them — that's a Partiful event waiting to be made official.

Collab ready 🍊 Poppi × Love Island

Poppi ran a Love Island collab this season and it was all over my feed. Watch parties are basically branded events waiting to happen and Villa Mixer watching parties co-sponsored with Poppi would be a natural extension.

Sound · Community 🎧 Soul Ties & Sound

NYC's underground music scene is having a moment: intimate sets, word-of-mouth venues, no headliners. Curating these onto Explore makes Partiful the discovery layer for the city's most culturally alive nights.
